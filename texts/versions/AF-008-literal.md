Chapter 1. The Writer's Object
Since I see thee, most excellent Diognetus, exceedingly desirous to learn the mode of worshipping God prevalent among the Christians, and wishing to make a clear and intelligent inquiry respecting them... I ask of thee to give earnest attention to what I say, and to become a disciple of the truth.
