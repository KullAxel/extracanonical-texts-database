Chapter 1. We Ought to Think Highly of Christ.
Brethren, it is fitting that we should have a sound mind respecting Jesus Christ as God, and as our Judge. For we ought not to think lightly of our salvation. For if we think little of Him, we shall also hope to obtain but little from Him.
