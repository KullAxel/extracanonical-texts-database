## Text

1 Clement - Simple English Version

Chapter 1
The church in Rome to the church in Corinth.

Grace and peace to you from God our Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.

Dear friends, because of the sudden troubles that have come on us, we have not been able to write to you as quickly as we wanted.
