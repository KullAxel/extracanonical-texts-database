## Text

The Didache (Teaching of the Twelve Apostles) - Literal Public Domain Translation (Roberts-Donaldson base)

Chapter 1. The Two Ways; The First Commandment.
There are two ways, one of life and one of death, but a great difference between the two ways. The way of life is this: First, you shall love God who created you; second, your neighbour as yourself, and do not do to another what you would not want done to you.
