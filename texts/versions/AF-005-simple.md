## Text

The Shepherd of Hermas - Simple English Version

Hermas has visions from an angel who appears as a shepherd. The visions teach about repentance, the church as a tower being built, and instructions for Christian living: avoid double-mindedness, be generous, and prepare for trials. It emphasizes that there is still time to repent.
