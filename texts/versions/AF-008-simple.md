## Text

Epistle to Diognetus - Simple English Version

The writer explains to Diognetus what Christians believe and how they live differently from Jews and pagans. Christians are in the world but not of it, like soul in body. They follow God\'s commands, show love, and their way of life reveals the goodness of their faith.
