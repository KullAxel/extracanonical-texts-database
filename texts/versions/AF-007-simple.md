## Text

Polycarp to the Philippians and Martyrdom - Simple English Version

Polycarp encourages the Philippians to live in faith, love, and good works, following the example of Christ and the apostles. The Martyrdom account describes how the elderly bishop was arrested and burned at the stake for refusing to deny Christ, showing remarkable courage and prayer.
