## Text

2 Clement - Simple English Version

Chapter 1. Value Jesus Highly
Brothers and sisters, we should take Jesus Christ seriously as our God and Judge. Don't take your salvation lightly. If you treat Jesus as unimportant, you can't expect much from Him in return.
