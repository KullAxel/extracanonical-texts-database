## Translation-access sources added in v0.3

- Florentino García Martínez and Eibert J. C. Tigchelaar, eds./trans., *The Dead Sea Scrolls Study Edition*, 2 vols. Brill/Eerdmans, 1997–1998. Best broad English access point for non-biblical Qumran texts; copyrighted.
- Albert Pietersma and Benjamin G. Wright, eds., *A New English Translation of the Septuagint*. Oxford University Press, 2007. Online access point: https://ccat.sas.upenn.edu/nets/edition/ . Rights require review before bulk hosting.
- James H. Charlesworth, ed., *The Old Testament Pseudepigrapha*, 2 vols. Doubleday, 1983–1985. Standard anthology for many Jewish pseudepigrapha; copyrighted.
- Michael W. Holmes, ed./trans., *The Apostolic Fathers: Greek Texts and English Translations*, 3rd ed. Baker Academic, 2007. Recommended modern study translation for Apostolic Fathers; copyrighted.
- Bart D. Ehrman and Zlatko Pleše, eds./trans., *The Apocryphal Gospels: Texts and Translations*. Oxford University Press, 2011. Recommended for apocryphal gospel material; copyrighted.
- J. K. Elliott, ed./trans., *The Apocryphal New Testament*. Oxford University Press, 1993; and Tony Burke et al., *New Testament Apocrypha: More Noncanonical Scriptures*. Recommended broad NT apocrypha access points; copyrighted.
- James M. Robinson, ed., *The Nag Hammadi Library in English*, revised ed. HarperOne, 1990; Bentley Layton, *The Gnostic Scriptures*. Recommended broad Nag Hammadi/Gnostic access points; copyrighted.
