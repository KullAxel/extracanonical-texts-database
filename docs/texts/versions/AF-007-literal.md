## Text

Polycarp to the Philippians and Martyrdom of Polycarp - Literal Public Domain Translation

Polycarp to the Philippians
Polycarp and the presbyters with him, to the Church of God sojourning at Philippi: Mercy to you, and peace from God Almighty, and from the Lord Jesus Christ, our Saviour, be multiplied.

Martyrdom of Polycarp (excerpt)
The Church of God which sojourns at Smyrna, to the Church of God sojourning in Philomelium, and to all the congregations of the Holy Catholic Church in every place: Mercy, peace, and love from God the Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ, be multiplied.
