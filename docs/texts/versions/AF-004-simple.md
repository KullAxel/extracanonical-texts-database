## Text

Epistle of Barnabas - Simple English Version

The letter begins with a greeting and explains that the writer wants to share deeper understanding of the faith. It discusses how the Jewish laws and sacrifices pointed to Christ, and urges believers to live in a new way, not following the old patterns but embracing the covenant through Jesus.
