To the Ephesians (excerpt)
Ignatius, who is also called Theophorus, to the Church which is at Ephesus in Asia, most blessed in the fullness of God the Father... I have become acquainted with your name, which is beloved by God, and have become acquainted with your love in Christ Jesus.
