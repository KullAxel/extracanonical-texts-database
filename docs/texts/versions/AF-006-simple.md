## Text

Epistles of Ignatius of Antioch - Simple English Version

Ignatius writes while being taken to Rome to be martyred. He urges churches to stay united around their bishop, avoid false teachers, and follow the example of Christ. He is eager to die for his faith and become "pure bread" for God.
